specimen sheet · macOS Terminal.app

Terminal Palette

Twenty themes — twelve dark, eight light, five of them backed by real texture (paper, linen, canvas, charcoal, denim). Every color in every theme is contrast-checked against its background — nothing disappears, not even ANSI black on dark or yellow on cream. Double-click a .terminal file to install, or run install.sh.
